About Me

Hi! My name is Kristin (she/her), and I am a third-year Computational Media major who is currently attending the Georgia Institute of Technology. Computational media is essentially a combination of computer science and liberal arts. The interdisciplinary nature of this major is what drew me towards it, and it offers numerous opportunities for how it can be applied. For instance, animation, graphic design, and game development fall under the category. Over the course of my time here, I have been learning how to code in Python, Java, and more. By using my artistic skills in conjunction with my technical skills, I aspire to do equitable software development post-graduation.
